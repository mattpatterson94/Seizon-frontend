Who Is Seizon?

Seizon is a collaboration between insanely good friends and co-workers Matt Patterson & Jordan Gardiner.

Working at a web development company for a number of years made us realize that, while there are some pretty awesome general solutions out there, there's nothing tailored for the web development industry.

In April of 2015 we decided to change that, and ever since we've been tinkering away at Seizon in our spare time.
